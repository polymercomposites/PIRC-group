Biography

Dr. Eric Asare is a Lecturer at Materials Engineering at KNUST. He has expertise in polymer composite design, processing and characterisation for particular applications with over 5 years industrial experience. Dr. Asare has experience in commercialisation of research work and have worked with companies and factories in United States, Asia and Europe. He has several papers in peer reviewed high impact journals and has presented at several conferences.

Research Interests

  • Smart multifunctional composites
  • Polymer processing and characterisation
  • Nanomaterials

    • Research Group

    Dr. Eric Asare leads the Advanced Composite Materials Research Group at KNUST, focusing on the development of smart multifunctional composites and polymer processing techniques.
