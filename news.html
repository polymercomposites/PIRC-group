News

July 2025: New Publication

Our latest paper, "Synergistic effects of micro-and macro-sized palm kernel shell fillers on the tensile properties of HDPE composites," was published in the Royal Society Open Science.

June 2025: Award

Congratulations to Jephtah for winning the gold award at the Vice Chancellor's Excellence Award. This recognition highlights our dedication to innovation and excellence in our projects.

June 2024: Successful Completion of SEESA Project

We are proud to announce the successful completion of the SEESA project focused on developing affordable and sustainable sanitary pads. This project aligns with our mission to promote health and well-being in underserved communities.

October 2023: KReF Award

Our team has been honored with the KReF research grant, recognizing our contributions to sustainable materials development. This is a testament to our commitment to making a positive impact and driving impactful change.
