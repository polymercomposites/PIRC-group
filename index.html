Welcome to PIRC

At Polymer Innovation Research and Consultancy, we assist clients in selecting the most suitable polymers for their specific applications. Our team of experts has extensive knowledge of polymer properties, cost, and performance requirements, ensuring that clients receive tailored solutions that meet their needs.

Our services include:

  • Optimized materials selection and development
  • Polymer processing and manufacturing
  • Polymer material characterization and testing
  • Research and development of new polymer materials
  • Consultation on polymer applications and performance
  • Collaboration with industry partners for innovative solutions

We are committed to delivering high-quality results and helping our clients achieve their goals.
