Welcome to PIRC

At Polymer Innovation Research and Consultancy (PIRC), we design new polymer and polymer composite materials and also assist our clients in selecting suitable polymer materials for their specific applications. Our team of experts has extensive knowledge in polymer properties, cost, and performance requirements, ensuring that clients receive tailored solutions that meet their needs.


Our services include:

  • Optimized materials selection and development
  • Polymer processing and manufacturing
  • Polymer material characterization and testing
  • Research and development of new polymer materials
  • Consultation on polymer applications and performance
  • Collaboration with industry partners for innovative solutions

    • We are committed to delivering high-quality results and helping our clients achieve their goals
